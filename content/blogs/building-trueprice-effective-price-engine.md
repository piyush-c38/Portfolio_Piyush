We have all been there.

You see a giant discount banner, a coupon prompt, a cashback offer, and a payment-card deal all at once. The simple question becomes surprisingly hard to answer: what is the actual amount I am going to pay?

That question became the starting point of TruePrice. The project aims to analyze every available offer, normalize the rules, and calculate the final effective price in a way that is understandable to the user.

The hardest part was not calculating discounts. It was teaching software to understand how modern e-commerce websites present them.
